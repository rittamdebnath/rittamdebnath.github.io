About the Project

Devoted is a temple app that allows devotee to find nearby temples, learn more about that temple, interact with them give reviews about their experience. Devotees can also become a temple member or a pujari for the temple and people who would like to participate in the day to day activities of the temple can become volunteer.

This app helps users to learn three different aspects i.e Spirituality, Religious and wellness. As of now we would be focusing on the religious part.

My Role

BI designed this app from the ground up. Working closely with Sridhar (CEO of Virility Solutions Pvt Ltd) to achieve our goals.

Designing this app was a huge challenge due to the time constraints and business objectives. We had a discussion regarding the way we are going to introduce this app in the market. Design Strategy

Understand the Problem and Creating Persona

According to Wikipedia, there are 78.9 percent of Indians who follow Hinduism in India. Due to which there are more than 400,000 temples and twice the number of Temple members who take part in the day to day activities in worshipping gods. So we decided to concentrate on one religion first and then add other religions to our app.

We also found that people when they visit temple they wear clothes which are usually orange, yellow or white in color. This color combination gave me the idea to choose Orange like gradient as a Primary color combination.

Characters and their pain points:

Ramesh is 48 years old, he is a very religious man takes part in the daily activities held at his local temple. He want to be connected to the temple members so that he doesn’t miss any of the programmes. He uses few bhajan apps.

Venu Gopal is 18 years old, his father is a pujari at the temple. He also wants to be a pujari one day, so he assists his father in every way possible in the puja and yajna that happens in the temple. He also conducts festivals inside the temple but doesn’t have any efficient way to call his friends and loved once to the festival. As of now he uses apps like facebook and whatsapp to get connected.

Identifying the User needs

DEVOTEES NEEDS
  • Devotees wanted to quickly navigate through the app.
  • Search Temple easily
  • Discover various activities that are currently organised by the temple
  • Participate in those activities
  • Connect with the Pujaris or any temple member
  • Know various things like astrology, Daily Mantra or answer quiz questions
  • Would like to receive Healthy tips
TEMPLE MEMBER NEEDS
  • Should have access to the temple details
  • Should be able to add or remove any volunteer
  • Invite a Volunteer
  • Chat with other temple members
  • Setup events for the temple
  • Socialize with devotees and other people in the network
